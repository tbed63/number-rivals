How to Play

  1. You are shown 3 random numbers. Pick one by tapping it.
  2. The operation for your mode is shown — apply it to your number mentally.
  3. Find the answer on the board and tap it to claim that square.
  4. Tap the wrong square and you lose your turn!
  5. First player to get 4 in a row (across, down, or diagonal) wins!
  6. 🔄 Reroll — get 3 completely new numbers (2× per game).
  7. ⏱ Restart Timer — reset your countdown this turn (once per game).
