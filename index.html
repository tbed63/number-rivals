How to Play

  1. You are shown 3 random numbers. Pick one.
  2. Pick an operation: Make 10, Make 100, Double, or Halve.
  3. The result is calculated. Find that number on the board and click it to claim it.
  4. Click the wrong cell and you lose your turn!
  5. First player to get 4 in a row wins!
  6. 🔄 Reroll — get 3 completely new numbers.
  7. 🔀 Swap — replace one of your 3 numbers with a new one.
  8. ⏱ Steal — steal 25% of your opponent's timer for their next turn.
